Why it exists

Why Salah Now

Prayer is a moment to step away from the noise. Many apps built around it have grown busier over time — more to sign into, more to notice, more happening quietly in the background.

Salah Now was made for the opposite reason. It does a few things with care — accurate times, an honest Qibla, gentle reminders, and a private record kept only for you — and then it steps back. The calm is the point.

Now

Quietly knowing where you are in the day

Open the app and the current prayer is simply there — its name, how long it has been, and what comes next. The arc of the day rests above it, calm and unhurried.

No setup and no noise. Just the time, the prayer, and room to breathe.

Qibla

A direction you can trust

The Qibla is drawn from a verified location fix, and the app tells you plainly when the reading is steady. It stays honest about accuracy, and never guesses.

Compass trusted — hold your phone flat and turn until the arrow points forward.

Reflection

A record for reflection, not performance

Mark prayers for yourself and look back gently over the weeks and months. There are no scores, no streaks, and no one to answer to but yourself.

Quietly looking back — nothing is shared, nothing is ranked, nothing is measured against anyone.

Reminders

On time, and then silent

Reminders arrive calmly when a prayer begins, and then they leave you alone. No guilt, no pressure, no streak to protect — only a gentle nudge you can shape to suit you.
